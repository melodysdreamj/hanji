# Hanji brand assets

`hanji-hero.webp` (illustration) and `hanji-mark.webp` (silhouette mark) are the
Hanji project's identity artwork, provided by the project owner. Unlike the
source code, they are **not** covered by the repository's MIT license — do not
reuse them to represent other projects or products. Derived raster sizes
(favicons, PWA icons, `og-image.jpg` under `web/public/`) are generated from
these masters.
